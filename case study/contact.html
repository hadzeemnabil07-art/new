Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a court?

You can book courts online through our website, via phone call, or visit us directly at the front desk.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before booking. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee.

Do you provide equipment?

Yes! We offer rackets, balls, shuttlecocks, and paddles for rental at the front desk.

Is parking available?

Free parking is available for all members and visitors. We have 200+ parking spots.

Are there shower facilities?

Yes, we have clean changing rooms with hot showers and lockers available.

Can I host events here?

Absolutely! Contact our events team for corporate events, tournaments, and parties.
